Seoul Average Bike Rentals per Hour on Weekdays in 2018

This project analyzed the average hourly bicycle rentals in Seoul on weekdays during 2018 using Tableau for data visualization. This visualization provides deep insight into bicycle usage patterns based on time of day, helping to understand trends that bicycle rental service providers can leverage in improving their operational strategies. Data is presented interactively to facilitate exploration and data-based decision making.
