Decision Support System for Best Employee Evaluation

This project was developed as a final thesis project, implementing the SMART (Simple Multi Attribute Rating Technique) algorithm. It is designed to assist in evaluating and selecting the best employees by providing a structured decision-making system based on various criteria and weighted attributes. The system aims to enhance objectivity, efficiency, and accuracy in employee performance assessment.
